### Reflecting on Failure in Group Work

In this unit, I learned about reflecting on failure through a group activity. This was a different experience for me because it was not only about studying, but also about working with others and understanding our mistakes.

During the group work, not everything went as expected. Sometimes we had different ideas, and it was difficult to manage time and divide the work equally. At first, I felt a bit uncomfortable because I wanted everything to go perfectly. But slowly, I understood that small failures are a normal part of teamwork.

The presentation was another challenging part. I felt nervous while presenting in front of others. There were moments where I was not fully confident about what I was saying. However, after completing it, I felt that I had improved my communication skills.

The viva was also an important experience. It tested my understanding and not just memorization. When I was unable to answer some questions clearly, I realized the areas where I needed to improve. Instead of feeling bad, I started thinking about how I could prepare better next time.

This unit taught me that failure is not something negative. It helps us learn and improve. By reflecting on what went wrong, I was able to understand my weaknesses and work on them.

Overall, this experience helped me grow not only in my subject, Information Science, but also in teamwork, communication, and confidence. It showed me that learning comes from both success and failure.
